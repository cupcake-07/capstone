About the Event

Buwan ng Wika (Language Month) is an annual celebration in the Philippines held every August. It highlights the importance of the Filipino language as a tool for national unity, cultural identity, and freedom.

This year, we celebrate our heritage through traditional dances (Sayaw), poetry (Balagtasan), and the wearing of our national costumes, the Baro't Saya and Barong Tagalog.
